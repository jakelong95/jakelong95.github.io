Education Objective

During my career as a student at Iowa State, I have learned as much about software engineering as possible through the classes I have taken. During my college career I also took several general education classes to expand my perspective beyond just Software Engineering and to improve my non-technical skills. These classes will give me insight into different problems facing the world, and will allow me to take these into account when designing software solutions that will reach a wide audience of users.

My General Education Classes

Class Number Class Name How it Helped
Relig 205 Introduction to World Religion This class helped me understand the differences between different cultures and religions, and why different religions believe what they do. This will help me work with people who hold different views than myself, because I will understand where their beliefs come from.
