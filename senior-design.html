Expresso

Expresso is a coffee bean subscription service that aims to bring local coffee roasters and consumers together. Users will be able to subscribe to monthly deliveries of their favorite roasts and discover completely new roasts.

My Part

I will be working on part of the back end server, which will be written in Go.

Why It Matters

Our project matters because it will allow smaller roasterse to be more competitive with international chains, such as Starbucks. This will help grow local economies and bring more competition to the marketplace.
